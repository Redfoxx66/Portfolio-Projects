Home

Welcome to the Box Office Database. Here you can find all the information you need on movies, actors, and user data. Using the navbar you can locate all of the individual pages that have specified information. This site is no longer in development and all functionalites are fully available. In the movies menu, you will see a list of movies and have the ability to look for genres or create your own. In the users menu, you will be able to create a user or look at the available users. In the actors menu, you will see a list of actors and can create your own actors and link up to any movie.
